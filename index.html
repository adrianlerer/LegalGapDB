How It Works

Our structured approach to documenting legal gaps enables researchers and practitioners to understand real-world legal practice.

1

Document Formal Rule

Identify the official legal requirement with citations to statutes, regulations, or case law.

2

Quantify Informal Practice

Gather empirical data on actual compliance rates from government statistics, surveys, or academic studies.

3

Explain Gap Mechanisms

Analyze WHY the gap persists - enforcement capacity, economic incentives, social norms, etc.
